The Masterway Extension for WooCommerce allows you to invoice your clients via Masterway.

== Description ==
The Masterway Extension for WooCommerce allows you to invoice your clients via Masterway.<br><br>
Main features:<br>
- Choose to generate the documents you need: invoice, simplified invoice or proforma invoice (draft).<br>
- Portuguese VAT Region configuration.<br>
- Configure the serie you want to generate your invoices.

== Installation ==

1. Place 'woo-masterway' folder in your plugins directory.
1. Activate the plugin.
1. Visit the Masterway settings under the WooCommerce menu and configure the required settings.
